Descriptors infrastructure

Descriptors are a special kind of Processing Data (see 3.2.2) that have proven very useful and necessary for many applications mainly related to audio content processing. At this moment, CLAM offers a limited infrastructure for computing and using descriptors related to the Processing Data. In many of our internal project we have seen the need for a more exhaustive solution to this problem that will address issues such as its efficient computation, storage, representation, retrieval...
