Analysis Oriented

Analysis oriented frameworks are audio processing frameworks with a clear focus on extracting features from an incoming audio signal. Although some other frameworks that will be seen in other categories may also include audio analysis tools, two particular frameworks, Marsyas and Maate, have an exclusive focus on such applications.
