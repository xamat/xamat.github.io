About the Agile Research Manifesto



This is a draft manifesto that intends to adapt the principles of the Agile Manifesto to Scientific Research. This is a very first draft version put up for discussion. If you want to contribute, please contact Xavier Amatriain (xavier at amantriain dot net) or join the Agile Research LinkedIn group.
