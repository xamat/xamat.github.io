Title:"Frameworks Generate Metamodels"
In: Report pending publication
Year: 2007
Abstract:

The benefits of modeling languages have been known for years in engineering. The basic idea is to come up with a set of commonly accepted concepts, notations, and rules in order to better express problems and solutions. This way we are mimicking the flexibility of a general language in which a vocabulary, syntax, and grammar allows to express complex ideas by combining atomic semantic and functional units. (...)
